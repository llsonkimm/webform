About The Author

The author of this site is an IT enthusiaist and majors in Software Development. He started out by pursuing a Bachelors Degree in Business Information Technology from Taita Taveta University and graduated on 27th November 2019 with a Second Class Honors. During the course of his study, he also worked as a cyber attendant for 2 years after having taken an academic leave and later undertook an attachment at Vipingo Ridge Ltd as IT Trainee and got hands-on experience in Newtwork Configuration and Management. Back in college, he was an active member of Mictec Club that was dedicated to enhancing collective training in Software Development by engaging with the already experienced personel in the field of Software developement. Through the club, he also got to participate in setting and administering of Math Contest in the Coastal Region which was also a big success and got certified for the partticipation.

Before and after completion of his studies, the author of this site has continously taken self training studies to improve on his Software Development prowess, as he clearly knows where his passion is.

Fast forward to present, he is now building this site for his present and future collection of portfolios. He now possess some skills he acquired from his personalised training as a python-Django developer for a month plus having began from zero knowledge with the said programming language.
